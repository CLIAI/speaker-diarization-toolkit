# Speechmatics Supported Languages and Models

## Operating Points

Speechmatics offers two accuracy models:

* **Standard**: "optimized for faster turnaround with strong accuracy"
* **Enhanced**: "our highest-accuracy model with strong turnaround times"

## Transcription Languages (60+)

The platform supports extensive language coverage including:

**Major Languages**: English, Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, Arabic, Portuguese, Russian, Japanese, Korean, Hindi, Bengali, and Thai.

**European Languages**: Polish, Italian, Dutch, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Finnish, Czech, Hungarian, Romanian, Greek, Bulgarian, Croatian, and others.

**Asian Languages**: Cantonese, Indonesian, Malay, Tamil, Tagalog, Vietnamese, Uyghur, and Mongolian.

**Additional**: Hebrew, Persian, Turkish, Ukrainian, Swahili, Welsh, and specialized options like Esperanto and Interlingua.

Each language uses ISO639-1 (two-letter) or ISO639-3 (three-letter) codes for API integration.

## Full Language List

Speechmatics supports 55+ languages and dialects for speech-to-text transcription:

Arabic, Bashkir, Basque, Belarusian, Bengali, Bulgarian, Cantonese, Catalan, Croatian, Czech, Danish, Dutch, English, Esperanto, Estonian, Finnish, French, Galician, German, Greek, Hebrew, Hindi, Hungarian, Indonesian, Irish, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Latvian, Lithuanian, Malay, Maltese, Mandarin, Marathi, Mongolian, Norwegian, Persian, Polish, Portuguese, Romanian, Russian, Slovak, Slovenian, Spanish, Swahili, Swedish, Tagalog, Tamil, Thai, Turkish, Ukrainian, Urdu, Uyghur, Vietnamese, Welsh.

## Multilingual Capabilities

Six supported bilingual/multilingual packs enable simultaneous transcription:

* Malay & English
* Mandarin & English
* Tamil & English
* Tagalog & English
* Mandarin, Malay, Tamil & English
* Spanish & English (requires domain configuration)

## Healthcare Domain

Medical-specific models available for: English, Danish, Dutch, Finnish, French, German, Norwegian, and Spanish. These models must use the enhanced operating point.

Medical transcription models provide unparalleled accuracy for medical use cases such as ambient scribes and dictation tools. These models are kept up to date using officially maintained data sources, bringing significant improvements in recognition of medical terminology.

## Language Identification

Language ID is supported for all of Speechmatics' languages except:

* Interlingua (ia)
* Esperanto (eo)
* Uyghur (ug)
* Cantonese (yue)
* Irish (ga)
* Maltese (mt)
* Urdu (ur)
* Bengali (bn)
* Swahili (sw)

## Translation Limitations

Some languages are currently unsupported for translation:

Arabic, Bashkir, Belarusian, Welsh, Esperanto, Basque, Interlingua, Mongolian, Marathi, Tamil, Thai, Uyghur, Cantonese.
